## Seasonal 3D Visualization

The visualization depicts three-dimensional illustration of the seasonal fluctuations in wind generation across California. It showcases power generation projects with a capacity of 10 MW or more, presenting their respective capacity factors (CF) on a base map that outlines the wind resources. The visualization incorporates monthly data for projects of this scale.
